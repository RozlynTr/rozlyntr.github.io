Annual Preston Community Park

Thanksgiving Food Drive

The Annual Preston Community Park Thanksgiving Food Drive will be held on Saturday, November 20th starting at 11:00 am. Your generosity is needed now more than ever! To help families have a thanksgiving meal this season, we are asking for donations of any nonperishable food items.

Donations may include: turkeys, pies, canned vegetables, canned yams, boxed stuffing, boxed mashed potatoes, desserts, and more!

All donations need to be dropped off by November 20th at the park or you can email the address listed below to get information about needing to drop off food items prior to the 20th. We are also looking for any volunteers that would like to stay and help us sort and organize the food items as well as deliver them to the food bank. Thanks for your love and support to give everyone in our community the holiday they deserve!
