This is How We Roll

Picture it now... you're riding one of our fun and affordable scooters with the sea breeze blowing through your hair. As you take in stunning vistas of the turquoise ocean, pristine sandy beaches, lush foilage, and exotic wildlife, your favorite person is seated right behind you, holding on tight!

Come to Cozumel Island, off the coast of Mexico in the western Caribbean Sea! Scoots has everything you need to get around.

Found in two convenient locations: Playa del Carmen - Cozumel Ferry Dock and the Terminal Puerta Maya.

What do we offer? There's lots of options to choose from! You can rent a Honda Metro, Honda Dio, or Honda PCX scooter. For a bigger group, rent a Honda Pioneer or a 2-4 door Jeep Wrangler. Go to rentals to learn more OR visit Reservations to book your sweet ride today!
