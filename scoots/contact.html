What You Should Know Before Traveling to Cozumel

When you vacation in Cozumel the odds are that you’ll go home utterly relaxed and with memories of spectacular scenery and friendly people. A 34-mile-long by 11-mile-wide island off of Mexico's Riviera Maya, Cozumel is quite unlike anywhere else. It’s largely undeveloped, with expanses of jungle and untouched shoreline that are a rarity on the mainland. Travelers don’t come for the partying, boisterous beach bikini volleyball games, or designer shopping. Instead, it's all about unplugging and soaking in the sun.

There's also plenty to see and do in Cozumel aside from the beach. The city of San Miguel has a laid-back vibe, and there are stunning stretches of untouched nature to be found along the eastern and southern shores.
