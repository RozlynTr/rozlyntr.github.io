Information and Services

  • $50 security deposit (credit card only) required to confirm reservations.**
  • Valid drivers license in hand required at time of rental.
  • No one under 12 years of age allowed to ride on scooters.
  • Fuel charge not included.
  • Unlimited mileage.
  • Liability insurance, free of charge.
  • Reservations' rate requires 24 hour prior notification.

  • Rentals may be returned to either location.
