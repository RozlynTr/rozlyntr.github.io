Color Scheme

The color scheme for the SE Idaho News website utilizes primarily a monochromatic color scheme comprised of shades of off-black, grey, and white as well as a slight complimentary color variation with yellow and dusty blue. The main background incoporates a faded and slightly desaturated yellow while the main body is white with grey text. The hex codes of the five colors are #231f20, #f5ad41, #ffffff, #404040 and #1E3F59. I chose a yellow accent with monochromatic hues because yellow (as well as blue) is one of the first colors that comes to mind when I think of weather (sunshine) but the pages also needed monotone colors incorporated so that the yellow isn't overbearing and hard to look at. Also, I built my logo before I started the main pages and focused the site colors around the logo design.
