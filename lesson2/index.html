Site Purpose

The main purpose of Southeast Idaho News and Weather is to provide reliable and accurate news and information for the Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven area. The information provided will include news articles for each city, county events in the area, pages dedicated to each city, a storm center, and a gallery page. The goal of webpage is to keep the community informed by displaying local stories that impact lives and providing a medium to advertise events and weather.

Home Page

The home page will have a current weather story, the photo of the day, and sections for each town. The information sections include breaking news headlines, article titles, and upcoming events scheduled in the town. An image of a prominent location in town will be displayed in each section.

City Pages

Three pages will each be dedicated to Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. Each page will have town-specific weather forecasts, local news, relevant articles, and an event calendar. The pages will also provide information on local attractions for each city. Individuals or groups will be able to advertise events that they are hosting scheduled for each town.

Storm Center

The Storm Center will provide in depth weather stories and educational information regarding the local climate. Important information will be posted on the Storm Center page when severe weather is threatens the region. Closures of schools and cancellations of events due severe weather conditions will be posted in the Storm Center page.

Gallery

The Gallery will provide a medium for the community members to upload images that they capture during everyday activities or special events. The user will be asked to provide a brief description of the image, including location and background information.

Site Domain

The site domain will be www.idahosoutheast.org. The site domain was searched for on Google Domains and verified that it is available.
