Target Audience

The target audience will primarily be adults, more specifically individauls between the ages of 25-65. The primary users are people looking for a reliable and accurate information covering their town and surrounding county areas. The reason I am "limiting" the audience to adults is because generally, older individuals are more interested about current events and stay caught up on local news than a younger audience.

We also expect to have users with a wide range of experience with technology. Therefore, the website will need to be user friendly for people with little experience with technology.

The median age of the three counties is about 36 years old.

The median income is approximately $57,000 annually. With this information, we expect most of our users to come from households with adults and young children. We expect that they will be looking for events which are family friendly.

Persona 1: Susan

Susan is a 42 year old married mother of five kids. She is an elementary school special education teacher in Preston, ID. Susan relies on Southeast Idaho News to provide accurate weather forecasting and information on local events for her family to attend as well as to plan her teaching schedule around (potential field trips for her class, outdoor vs indoor activities, etc.) Her kids are Rose (20), David (18), Bell (15), Wilden (12) and Elizabeth (9). The younger kids enjoy the local sports such as Little League and soccer and the older kids are always wanting to go to local events such as school football games or fundraisers. When Susan has time in her crazy life, she enjoys going on hikes and being outdoors with her family. On these outings, she enjoys taking photos of weather phenomenon especially thunderstorms blowing in over mountains.

Persona 2: Kyle

Kyle is a 26 year old bachelor that enjoys being outdoors more than indoors. He is a construction site manager for a local residential construction company that serves the tri-county area. He recently graduated with a bachelor's degree in construction management. Now he enjoys his extra time since he longer attends college. When he is not at work, Kyle can be found out in the local forest or at a local lake. He enjoys backpacking, camping, fishing, and hunting. He relies strongly on accurate weather forecasts. When he plans his activities, he utilizes the forecast to ensure that he is prepared for the weather. He enjoys sharing his outdoor experiences with others and writes a weekly article about the local outdoor activities that are available in the area.

Scenario 1

Where can someone find information regarding scheduled community events either in a county or city?

Each city page will include a calendar with scheduled events for that community. Also, contact information for the local tourist office will be provided.

Scenario 2

Can I get notifications of severe weather, closures, or cancellations due to weather events?

The Storm Center page will provide information regarding severe weather, closures, or cancellations due to severe weather. If you subscribe with Tri-County News, we can provide severe weather alerts via push notifications based on your zip code.

Scenario 3

My husband's childhood friend and her husband will be coming to town for the weekend. What events are happening this weekend? What attractions are there in the area for young adults and people that have never been to southeastern Idaho?

Each city page will have a calendar of events. Also, the tourist office for each town often has lists of attractions in their city. The contact information for the tourist office can be found on each city page. Google Maps are embedded into the website. The user can utilize this feature to search for attractions in the area of interest.

Scenario 4

What are the most popular restraurants in each town or the region?

A freelance contributor writes an article every two weeks about her critiques of local restraurants. A user can find this article on the home page of the website.

Scenario 5

I took several pictures this weekend of our outing at Bear Lake near Fish Haven. Can I upload those photos?

Photos can be uploaded to the Gallery page of the website. A simple form will need to be completed to provide some basic contact information.
